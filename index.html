About me

Dr. Alina Kirshon-Goldman, DMA, CPC

Certified life coach, educator, and researcher passionate about wellness, growth, and transformation.

I believe we all have the power to create the life we truly want. As a coach, I help clients clarify their values, challenge limiting beliefs, and develop the mindset and strategies needed to move forward with confidence. My approach combines deep listening, curiosity, and iPEC's foundational principles to raise awareness of how values shape thoughts, emotions, and actions.

I focus on helping clients define what life satisfaction means to them and provide practical tools to increase resilience, well-being, and fulfillment. Whether working with individuals or entrepreneurs, my goal is to create a safe, supportive space where growth and transformation can happen.

Alongside my coaching practice, I bring years of experience as an educator, entrepreneur, and music studio owner. I'm a Suzuki violin teacher and hold degrees from the Jerusalem Academy of Music and Dance and a doctorate in voice performance from Shenandoah Conservatory. Since 2009, I've taught and managed my own home-based studio in Herndon, Virginia, and previously directed programs in early childhood education and summer camps.
