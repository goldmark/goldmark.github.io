Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2025

Information We Collect

When you use our website or contact us, we may collect:

  • Name and contact information (email, phone)
  • Information you provide in forms or messages
  • Usage data through analytics tools (Google Analytics, Microsoft Clarity)

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and schedule consultations
  • Improve our website and services
  • Send occasional updates (with your consent)

Analytics and Cookies

We use Google Analytics and Microsoft Clarity to understand how visitors use our website. These services use cookies to collect anonymous data such as:

  • Pages visited and time spent on site
  • Device type and browser information
  • General location (city/region level)
  • User interactions (clicks, scrolling, session recordings)

You can opt out of these analytics tools using our cookie consent banner or through your browser settings.
